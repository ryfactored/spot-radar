UI Breakdown

  • Filter bar — pinned above feed. Segmented toggle for release type, dropdown for recency, "Mark all seen" button.
  • "New since" divider — teal accent, shows count + date of last check. Separates unseen from previously seen.
  • Expanded cards — album art (88px, rounded), title (Plus Jakarta Sans, bold), artist, type + date, track count badge on art. "Open in Spotify" gradient CTA + "Dismiss" ghost button.
  • Collapsed cards — slim row, 36px art, title + artist + type/date in one line, reduced opacity. Click anywhere to re-expand.
  • No borders — separation via surface color shifts per design system. Cards use surface-container-high, collapsed use surface-container.
  • First-time sync — feed populates progressively with a subtle "Syncing your library... 847 of 2,031 artists checked" indicator in the filter bar area.
